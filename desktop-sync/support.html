Give

Support Folklore.

Every dollar you give stays in the Tri-Cities literary community. Some people like knowing exactly what their gift does, some would rather we put it where it's needed. Both kinds of giving live here.

Choose where to give

Shelf Life

Put books on shelves.

Every quarter, readers vote on a book by a local or regional author. We buy the winner from a Tri-Cities indie bookstore and place copies in free libraries across the metro. Your $5 vote funds the whole thing. Voting opens quarterly, follow along so you don't miss the next one.

How Shelf Life works

Round Table

Take care of book club night.

When a Tri-Cities book club picks a regional author's book, we buy the copies from a local indie bookstore. Meeting somewhere without food service? We cover the snacks and drinks too. Refreshment sponsorships are running now; book requests open this fall.

How Round Table works

General Fund

Give where it's needed most.

We also run on the less-visible work: licensing fees, insurance, venue rentals, and the dozen small things that keep events going. A gift here lets us put your support wherever it's most useful at the moment.

Give to general operations

Author Fund

Bring writers to readers.

Your gift underwrites author visits to the Tri-Cities: honoraria, travel, lodging, and the small things that make a good author experience into a great one. $25 gets a visiting author a warm welcome. $500 brings a debut indie author out to read.

Give to the Author Fund

Dedication

Sponsor a bottle.

For $25, we'll label a bottle with your name and a dedication to whoever you're thinking of: a grandmother who read to you, a partner, a friend. It joins the bar lineup at our next event, and afterward the bottle is yours to keep if you want it.

Questions about giving? Reach us at hello@folkloresocietywa.org.

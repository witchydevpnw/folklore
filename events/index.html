The Freedom to Read

BAGG Books · Downtown Kennewick · Free & open to all

A day-long celebration of the freedom to read whatever you want — a free book swap, banned romance, bookish crafts, and a Folklore beer garden out back. Presented by Obsidian Book Collective, hosted by BAGG Books, with Folklore on the bar.

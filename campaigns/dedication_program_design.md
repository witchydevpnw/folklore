# Dedication — Program Design Document

Dedication is Folklore Society's $25 sponsored-bottle campaign. A donor contributes $25, Folklore applies a custom label with the donor's name (and optional dedicatee and short message), and the bottle appears in the bar lineup at the next Folklore event. Bottles are available for pickup after the event.

This document captures everything decided about the program. Launch copy (Zeffy, website, social, emails, label brief) is the next deliverable and will be drafted as a separate Dedication Launch Pack.

---

## What Dedication is

A low-ticket, emotionally warm way for supporters to contribute to Folklore's bar-event operations while getting a tangible, personal moment at the event.

The mechanic: donor gives $25. Folklore curates a bottle from existing or upcoming inventory. A custom label with the donor's name (and optional dedicatee + optional short message) is applied. The bottle appears in the bar lineup at the next Folklore event. A photo of the labeled bottle is posted on the website and in event social coverage. After the event, the empty bottle is available for pickup within 30 days from a Folklore contact.

The warmth comes from the "dedicated-to" mechanic. A donor can dedicate the bottle to someone: a grandmother who first put books in their hands, a reader who's gone, a partner, anyone. That turns a $25 gift into a small public tribute and makes the campaign inherently shareable.

## Locked decisions

- **Name:** Dedication
- **Price:** $25 per bottle
- **Bottle selection:** Folklore curates; donor doesn't choose
- **Label fields:** donor name (required), dedicated-to (optional), short message ~100 characters (optional)
- **Donor attendance:** not required
- **Pickup:** available for 30 days after the event, from a Folklore contact, arranged individually for now
- **Queue model:** rolling (donate any time, bottle appears at next scheduled event)
- **Launch window:** soft launch late April / early May 2026, ahead of June 8 first event
- **Attribution on bottles:** every label carries a small Folklore wordmark in one corner

## Operational workflow (high level)

1. Donor gives $25 via Zeffy, fills out label fields on the donation form
2. Data flows to Candace (or designated admin) for queueing
3. Before next event: bottle selected, label produced, applied
4. At event: bottle displayed and served, photographed for social and donor follow-up
5. Post-event: social post featuring dedicators, thank-you email to donor with photo of their labeled bottle
6. 30-day pickup window opens; after that, empty bottles are recycled or repurposed

## Attorney flag

Minimal. Dedication is straightforward donor recognition tied to events Folklore already has licensing for. No individual grants, no private-benefit concerns. Worth a quick pass on the label design and public copy before launch to confirm nothing inadvertently positions the bottles as "for sale" rather than "featured as donor recognition," a distinction that matters under WSLCB rules.

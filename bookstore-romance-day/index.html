What's happening✦

One day, all for the romance readers. BAGG Books is throwing open its doors for Bookstore Romance Day — joining indie bookshops across the country — and Folklore Society is pulling the taps out back. Expect ink, signings, cold drinks, and exactly zero shame about your to-be-read pile.

  • A Folklore beer garden out back — cold pours, good company, and room to linger
  • Flash tattoos by Carly Joy — wear your favorite trope on your sleeve, for real this time
  • Blake Gallows, in person — book signing plus a meet & greet with the author
  • More still to come — we're adding to the lineup, so keep an eye out

Folklore on the bar✦

Pull up a chair — the beer garden is Folklore's corner of the day. Every pour goes straight back into our general fund, which keeps the lights on across all our programs. Buy a round, fund a reading.

Want your gift to go somewhere specific? Our Author Fund is raising money right now to bring real, working writers to the Tri-Cities. Cash gifts are always welcome, and every dollar adds up.

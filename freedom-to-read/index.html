What's happening✦

A day put together to celebrate the simple, ongoing right to read whatever you want. Obsidian Book Collective presents, BAGG Books hosts, and Folklore Society is running the bar. We're all out for the same reason.

  • Banned romance pulled from BAGG's shelves, inside the store
  • A free book swap. Bring a few, take a few
  • Bookish crafts and temporary tattoos
  • Beer garden out back with music and yard games

Folklore on the bar✦

The beer garden is Folklore's contribution to the day. Every pour we serve goes back into our general fund, which keeps the lights on across all our programs.

If you'd rather give to something specific, our Author Fund is fundraising now to bring writers to the Tri-Cities. Cash gifts are always welcome.
